EAGLE

Eagles are some of the largest birds. They are at the top of the food chain, with some species feeding on big prey like monkeys and sloths. Eagles have amazing eyesight and can detect prey up to two miles away.

  • Scientific Name : Haliaeetus leucocephalus
  • Average Length : 182cm-229cm
  • Average Lifespan : 14 Years
  • Habitat : Seacoasts

The Eagle is an unforgettable animal. Its white head and tail, eight-foot wingspread, piercing eyes, massive hooked beak and powerful talons make it unmistakable among birds.Eagles are most often seen soaring on their broad, flat wings, or else perched conspicuously, often in the upper branches of tall trees.
